2015 killings by police officers in the US

African Americans represent 12.1% of the total population in the US.

However, they represent almost the 25% of the total people killed by police. This higher rate illustrates how African Americans are killed by police at a much higher rate than other populations

Percentage of African Americans killed out of total killed

The following graph shows by state how many of the people killed by police are African Americans. We can see how they are disproportionally killed by police in many states, particularly when we compare the percentage of the population they represent. Absolute number of total killings are provided, as rates provided might not be representative for states with fewer killings.

How White and African Americans are killed

We see some differences between races in terms of how people are killed. For example it looks like white people tend to have a higher number of tasered and shot than African Americans people, which could indicate that black people are shot directly without other interventions first.
