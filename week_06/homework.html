Police Killings by Ethnicity

African-Americans represent 12.1% of the US population while White people account for 57.8%.

However, the data shows that African-Americans are killed at a higher rate than other ethnicities. On the other hand, White people are killed at a lower rate than would be expected if ethnicity played no role in police killings.
